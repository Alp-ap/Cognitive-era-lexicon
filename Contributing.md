# Contributing to The Lexicon

This project is a living correction of the language used to describe the Cognitive Era. We welcome contributions that bring precision, architectural rigor, and philosophical honesty.

### How to Engage
1. **Entry I ([Open]):** We are looking for the definitive name to replace "Artificial Intelligence." Join the debate in [GitHub Discussions].
2. **Refinements:** If a proposed term lacks precision or an entry misrepresents a technical reality, please open an **Issue** or a **Pull Request**.
3. **Tone:** Avoid marketing hype or "doomer" narratives. We speak in terms of architecture, functions, and outcomes.

### Versioning
This is currently **Draft 0.9**. We will move to **Version 1.0** once Entry I is settled and the community has vetted the initial 13 definitions.
